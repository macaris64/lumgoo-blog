# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-10

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Pomodoro Application (“the app”) handles user data. The app follows a privacy-first and minimal data processing approach. We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring transparency.

## Information We Collect

The app does not collect any personal information from users.

We do not collect, store, or process:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location data
- Device identifiers

or any other personally identifiable information.

## Use of Local Storage

The app uses the device’s local storage solely to enhance user experience.

The locally stored data includes:
- Pomodoro session information
- Pomodoro history and statistics
- User preference settings

This data:
- Is stored only on the user’s device
- Is never shared with the developer or any third party
- Is not uploaded or transmitted outside the device

### Data Deletion

Users can delete all locally stored data at any time through the Settings section within the app.

## Advertising

The app may display in-app advertisements to support its free usage.

We use third-party advertising services. These services may collect certain data according to their own privacy policies.
However, our app does not provide or send any personal user data to these advertising platforms.

The advertising provider we use:

- Google AdMob

You can review Google’s Privacy Policy here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for users of all ages and does not contain sensitive or restricted content.
The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Data Security

Because the app does not collect personal data, there is no risk of personal information exposure.
All locally stored application data remains securely on the user’s device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page and will take effect upon publication.
